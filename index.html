Messi Decides to leave Barcelona

Lionel messi is holding firm to his decision to leave Barcelona in the present transfer window and join Manchester city - regardless of the insistence of Bartomeu, that he will only be sold at the full $700 million value of his release clause.

Since joining Barcelona Messi has been involved in a third of all goals scored by the team. His stats:

  • Appearances: 869
  • Goals: 704
  • Assists: 298

Manchester city are working on the details of a contract that will secure the Argentine's foward's status as the world's best-paid footballer.
